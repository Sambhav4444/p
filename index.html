About

My hobby is reading books. The youth fiction genre interets me most. Whenever I go to home from my school I like to read such books after completing my home work. I am 13 years old and study in class 7th standard. Now I know very well that reading is very good habit which can make me a complete. This hobby can be developed by anyone however I got this naturally. Reading books keep one always happy and busy. It is the good source of enjoyment, knowledge, inspiration and instruction. It makes us disciplined, loyal, punctual and most importantly a successful person in the life.

My Reading Journey

My reading journey started with "Goosebumps: Attack Of The Jack-O'-Lanterns" by R.L Stine. I finished it in one day I loved it. My journey continued with "Magnus Chase And The Hammer Of Thor" by Rick Riordan. The funny thing is that it's the second book in the trilogy but I liked it, so my Mom and Dad bought the next part, the third book in the trilogy "Magnus Chase And The Ship Of The Dead" by Rick Riordan and the first book in the trilogy "Magnus Chase And The Sword Of Summer" by Rick Riordan I decided to read the third book in the trilogy and then I read the first one. After I finished the trilogy of Magnus Chase, my Dad bought another book "Spooksville: The Haunted Cave" by Christopher Pike in the series there are 25 books it was the third book, I loved it so my Dad bought the first book "Spooksville: The Secret Path" by Christopher Pike and the second book "Spooksville: The Howling Ghost by Christopher Pike I fished both of them in 2 days, I will read the fourth soon.I bought "Goosebumps: Welcome To The Dead House". It was awsome and scary. The next book "The Subtle Knife" by Phillp Pullman, the second book of a trilogy the first book was not available, so I tried reading the second book but it was related to the first one. I couldn't understand what was going on, so I kept it for a while and soon my Mom and Dad bought me the first book "The Golden Compass" by Phillp Pullman and the third book "The Amber Spyglass" by Phillp Pullman I completed book one and I am planning to read the collector book of Magnus Chase "9 From The Nine Worlds".
